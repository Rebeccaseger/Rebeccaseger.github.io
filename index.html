TRAVEL DIARY: What not to miss at Oahu, Hawaii

When visting Oahu it is easy to get stuck at Waikiki beach, but the main island of Hawaii has so much more. Here is what you not should miss out of if you ever travel to Oahu:

1. Hanuma bay nature preserve, a snorkeling paradise along the south coast of Oahu. PS. come here early in the morning to avoid the largest congestion. 2. Hike Diamond Head or Koko Crater Trail, when visiting a island of Hawaii hiking is mandatory, because the view is out of this world. 3. North Shore, nothing is more famous on Oahu then the North Shore with it's enormous waves during winter. But the coast is worth driving during all seasons, with it's cozy little towns, beautiful beaches (not as crowded as Waikiki) and pineapplecultures. 4. Nu'uanu Pali Lookout, do not miss this fantastic lookout point. PS. Be aware of the bees.
